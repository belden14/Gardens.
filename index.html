About Gardens

We believe everyone can grow their own food, regardless of experience or space. Since 2020, we've been delivering carefully curated gardening boxes to homes across Europe, Asia, and New Zealand.

Our mission is to make gardening accessible, sustainable, and joyful. Whether you have a small balcony or a large allotment, we provide the seeds, tools, and knowledge to help you succeed.
